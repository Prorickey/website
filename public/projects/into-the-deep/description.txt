Before I arrived at NCSSM, I had never known that I could get involved with robotics. But ever since I've joined the RoboKnights at the beginning of junior year, I've been building systems that turn ideas into reality. Throughout my rookie year, I've learned how to create PID controllers, create controllable systems, implement vision systems, and more. Just recently at the 2025 Chicago Robotics Invitation, I implemented a camera vision system that leveraged on board computing to spot, identify, and locate game pieces on the field. My system even went as far as to move the arm to the game piece for pickup. I'm excited to be the captain of RoboKnights for the upcoming season, and hope to make strides in the FTC world.
