This app was designed to help connect the North Carolina DECA student officer board with the DECA students across the state. It's crucial that the student officers can quickly get information out to their districts and post invaluable resources for students to use to study and prepare for their events. Featured on the app is an authentication system, notifications, and resource tabs. I helped lead the development of the app, focusing on the backend infrastructure and frontend user experience. Kubernetes is used to host the database and backend, which is built with gin.
